OUMS Sponsor: Cafe Loco

(The Old Palace, 85/87 St Aldates, Oxford. Map) Just 5 minutes away from the centre of Oxford, Cafe Loco offers a wide range of specialty coffees and teas as well as wonderful options for lunch. With a cosy atmosphere and always welcoming staff, you can sit and enjoy a wonderful view of Christ Church and its meadow.

Our store in Oxford is in the centre of Oxford Covered Market – unit 38, Avenue 2 next to Dragon’s Den and opposite Pingui. We only stock handmade wooden gifts which are either made or designed by us. We do outsource abroad but always do this fairly knowing and respecting the many craftspeople who make our goods.
